About us

You are sitting where ever you are reading this article because you were wandering around looking for a platform to exersice what you have learnt and gain new knowledge because practice make everyone perfect. Well you are at the right place!

SoloQuiz, a website designed for users familiar with programming languages, consists of different questions from 5 basic programming languages in the form of a quiz.
Are you a beginner, an intermidiate or advanced? Don't worry! each language consists of questions that are catagorized under five levels. As a result, it allows you to start from the basics and upgrade yourself as you go further.

Every question has a timer set, which helps users to focus and stay commited once they start the quiz. Our website also keeps track of every user's result to make them compete with eachother and display rankings. Since you already know that exersicing daily is the master key on the programming language,just consider our website as a place to find that key.
